So basically my friend keeps falling for ugly assholes and gets her heart broken so for catharsis I just thought why not let her beat them up in a game. I drew this kinda pixel art thing for thie game. The idea is there's a ball running around and you're trying to hit it. There's a label/health bar at the top that shows the health of the ball and what you want to do is make it go down to zero. When it does hit zero, it will kill the ball and give the endscreen.

Controls:
A: Left
D: Right
S: Down
Space: Jump
E: Dash--when held with the left or right controls

I made my project with Godot and if anyone wants to contribute, please help me with the art I'm actually so bad at the art it's so sad yet so real for me myself. My art teacher once called me a devolved human because how crappy my art is ;-;
